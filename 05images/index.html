What is the dice making process like?

Prepare the Mold: Ensure your silicone dice mold is clean and dry. You can lightly spray it with a mold release agent to make it easier to remove the dice once cured.

Add Color: Once the resin is mixed, add dyes, glitter, or other colorants to achieve the desired look for your dice. Mix thoroughly.

Pour Resin Into Molds: Carefully pour the resin mixture into the silicone dice mold, filling each die shape. Leave a little space at the top to prevent overflow.

Cure the Resin: Let the resin cure according to the manufacturer's instructions. This can take several hours to a day, depending on the resin used.
